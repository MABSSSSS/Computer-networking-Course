**MAC ADDRESS:**

Media access control.

also known as physical address or hardware address.

MAC address is unique and permananta address for all electronic and networking devices.

MAC address format:

MM:MM:MM:SS:SS:SS

MM-MM-MM-SS-SS-SS

MMM.MMM.SSS.SSS

Example:

3C:D9:2B:6F:2b:9c

USE:

device tracking.
mac address
