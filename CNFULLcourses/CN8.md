**GATEWAY:**

It is hardware device that is used to connect two dissimilar type of network.
It allows us to send and receive data through internet even it is LAN network.


*NOTE:*
It operates all 7 layer of OSI 

*ADVANTAGE:*
It connects two network which has different protocol.
It operate all 7 layer of protocols.
We can not access the internet without gateway.
It provide some security.

Disadvantage:
It is more expensive.
Data transmission rate is slower.
Difficult to maintain as well as very complex.
It is less intelligent.
